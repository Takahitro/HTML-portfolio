The Best Movies According to Takahiro

My top 3 movies of all-time.

21 JUMP STREET

The pinnacle of comedy films. Recommended for everyone.

Social Network

Cool and funny. The story of Mark Zuckerberg creating Facebook.

Don't Look UP

A Netflix movie. It had an intriguing plot twist and was entertaining like never before. It made me contemplate how I would spend my last day on Earth.
